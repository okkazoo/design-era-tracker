What to do

  • Read texts like a writer, focusing on specific aspects of writing.
  • Use what you learn from your reading to improve your own writing.
  • Your reading responses will be the research for a connections essay.
  • You need to plan what aspects of your own writing you want to develop.

Key requirements

  • Focus on aspects of writing such as genre, setting, character types, purpose, ideas, dialogue, types of poetry, or narrative point of view.
  • Responses must be supported by evidence from the texts.
  • Develop personal responses to the texts.
  • The maximum word limit for your response is 600 words.

Tips

  • Look at the exemplar to understand what is expected.
  • Use the Overall reading to write Plan to organize your thinking.
  • Use the tables provided to keep track of your reading responses.

Dates & deadlines

  • DUE: Week 8, Term 3
