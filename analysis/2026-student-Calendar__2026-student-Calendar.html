What to do

This assignment is about fashion and textiles, focusing on design, pattern adaptation, and construction. You will explore different design eras, develop your own design ideas, learn advanced pattern adaptation techniques, and construct a final garment. You will also develop a portfolio showcasing your work.

Key requirements

  • Explore and use characteristics of a design movement or era to inform your design ideas.
  • Use visual communication techniques to generate design ideas.
  • Learn and apply advanced pattern adaptation techniques to change structural and style features of a design.
  • Implement advanced procedures using textile materials to make a specified product with special features.
  • Create a final garment.
  • Develop a portfolio that includes your design process, finished garment photos, and construction details.
  • Complete written assessments and presentations.

Tips

  • Use the scaffold to help organize your writing.
  • Research and writing for the Design Era assignment continues as homework.
  • Prepare your presentation draft in Week 6 of Term 1.
  • Your Design Era assignment presentation is due in Week 10 of Term 1.
  • Ensure your written brief and design are submitted in Week 3 of Term 2.
  • Your Pattern Adaptation assessment brief/design/process documentation/garment is due in Week 8 of Term 2.
  • Your Advanced Procedures work is due in Week 6 of Term 3.
  • Your External portfolio should have no more than 20 A3 pages.
